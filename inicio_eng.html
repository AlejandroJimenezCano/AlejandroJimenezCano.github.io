Presentation

Welcome to my personal webpage. My name is Alejandro Jiménez Cano, and I am a postdoctoral researcher. So far my research has been focused on the general properties and the structure of Metric-Affine Gauge gravity and other modified gravity theories, as well as the analysis of particular solutions. I am very interested in the study of their stability as reliable physical models, as well as the viability of the theories that are involved.

In addition, I usually take advantage of any opportunity to participate in informative talks and outreach activities with children, showing them some of the deepest aspects of the modern gravitational theories.
