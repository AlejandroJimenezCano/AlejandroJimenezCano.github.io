Presentation

Welcome to my personal webpage. My name is Alejandro Jiménez Cano, and I am a researcher and lecturer at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. So far my research has been focused on the general properties and the structure of theories of gravity beyond General Relativity. I have mostly worked on solutions and stability issues of various theories such as cubic gravity and theories formulated in the so-called Metric-Affine framework. I have many interests in the field of gravitation such as junction conditions, black hole stability, TDiff theories and differential geometry.

Regarding my teaching activity, I am part of the Unidad Docente de Física of the ETSI de Montes, Forestal y del Medio Natural and give classes on General Physics in the Degree in Natural Environmental Engineering and the Degree in Environmental Technologies Engineering.

In addition, I usually take advantage of any opportunity to participate in informative talks and outreach activities with children, showing them some of the deepest aspects of the modern gravitational theories.
